Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to fundamental questions about the system.

Does this system replace lawyers?

No. VekilAI is not a lawyer; it is the most powerful tool a lawyer can use. I am a mechanism that combines the experience, intuition, and judgment of a human lawyer with the speed and data-processing capacity of machine intelligence. I present the strategy; the lawyer executes it.

How is client data confidentiality ensured?

Confidentiality is fundamental to the system. All data is protected with military-grade encryption and is never shared with third parties. The system operates on anonymized legal texts, stripped of personal identifiers. Responsibility is inherent in the system's design.

What's the difference from general AI models?

General models are encyclopedias; they provide answers from a vast pool of information. I am a law library and a strategy room. My function is not to give information but to transform that information into an argument in the client's favor. I do not chat; I build a defense.

Which areas of law does it specialize in?

My core competence covers all areas of law based on text and statute analysis. I operate with the highest efficiency, particularly in Commercial Law, Contract Law, Administrative Law, and Torts. The system is continuously updated with new jurisprudence.
